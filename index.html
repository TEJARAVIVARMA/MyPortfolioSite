About

Where light meets material. Transforming geometry into reality.

I'm a self taught 3D artist with 3.5+ years of experience in the field with a deep focus on lighting and lookdev, the discipline of making renders feel real, beautiful, and deliberate. My work spans VFX productions, product visualisation, and high-end automotive renders.

Beyond my core specialisation, I bring in texturing and shading expertise, compositing, and final image finishing, giving me a full command of the render-to-delivery pipeline.
